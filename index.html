FAQ

Quick answers to common questions about Snap 2 Keep orders.

What kind of photos work best?
Clear, bright photos with good lighting work best. Avoid very dark screenshots or tiny, blurry images.
Can you edit or crop my photos?
Light cropping and basic adjustments are included. If a photo doesn’t work well for the format, I’ll let you know and suggest alternatives.
Do you accept custom bulk or event orders?
Yes. For markets, weddings, or group gifts, please reach out with your quantity, date, and product type so I can quote a custom bundle.
Are designs very colorful or more minimal?
Snap 2 Keep focuses on clean, modern layouts that highlight your photos. Soft colors and simple designs keep everything timeless.
